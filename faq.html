Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services, contracts, and policies

Why do you require a 6-month contract? +

Our 6-month contract ensures we can provide you with consistent, high-quality service and support. This timeframe allows us to:

  • Monitor your website's performance and make necessary optimizations
  • Provide regular security updates and maintenance
  • Build a strong working relationship with you
  • Ensure your website achieves its full potential in search rankings
  • Offer better pricing by spreading development costs over time

This commitment benefits both parties and ensures your website remains secure, fast, and effective.

What happens if I need to cancel before the contract ends? +

We understand that circumstances can change. If you need to cancel before your 6-month contract ends:

  • You'll need to provide 30 days written notice
  • Any remaining balance for the contracted period will be due
  • You'll retain full ownership of your website and all content
  • We'll provide you with all necessary files and access credentials
  • We'll assist with transferring your site to a new host if needed

We always try to work with our clients to find solutions that work for everyone.

What third-party services do you use and integrate? +

We work with various trusted third-party services to enhance your website's functionality:

  • Payment Processing: Stripe, PayPal, Square
  • Email Services: Mailchimp, ConvertKit, EmailJS
  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Google Search Console
  • Hosting: Netlify, Vercel, AWS
  • CDN: Cloudflare for improved speed and security
  • Forms: Formspree, Netlify Forms

All integrations are included in your package, and we handle the setup and configuration for you.

What is your refund policy? +

Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent:

  • First 14 days: Full refund if you're not satisfied with the initial design
  • After 14 days: Refunds are prorated based on services not yet provided
  • Monthly payments: No refund for the current month, but you can cancel future payments
  • Third-party costs: Domain registration and third-party service fees are non-refundable

We stand behind our work and will always try to resolve any issues before considering a refund.

Do I own my website and content? +

Yes, absolutely! You maintain full ownership of:

  • All content you provide (text, images, videos)
  • Your domain name (which you purchase separately)
  • Your website files and code after the initial contract period
  • All customer data and analytics

We simply provide the service of building, hosting, and maintaining your website. You're never locked in permanently.

What's included in the monthly maintenance? +

Your monthly maintenance fee covers comprehensive website care:

  • Security updates and monitoring
  • Performance optimization
  • Backup management
  • Content updates (up to 2 hours per month)
  • Technical support via email
  • Uptime monitoring
  • SEO maintenance and improvements
  • Analytics reporting

This ensures your website stays secure, fast, and up-to-date without you having to worry about technical details.

How long does it take to build my website? +

Timeline depends on the package and complexity:

  • Static Website: 1-2 weeks after content is provided
  • E-Commerce Package: 2-4 weeks depending on product catalog size
  • Custom Projects: Timeline discussed during consultation

We'll provide you with a detailed timeline and keep you updated throughout the process. The biggest factor in timeline is how quickly you can provide content and feedback.

Can I make changes to my website after it's built? +

Absolutely! We encourage you to keep your website fresh and updated:

  • Minor content updates are included in your monthly maintenance
  • We can train you to make basic updates yourself
  • Larger changes or new features can be added for additional fees
  • We provide ongoing support to help with any questions

Your website should grow with your business, and we're here to help make that happen.
